
Moosewood Pesto Recipe

Ingredients:
2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup olive oil
1/3 cup pine nuts
3 medium garlic cloves, minced
Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Combine the basil leaves, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and minced garlic in a food processor or blender.
2. Process until the mixture is smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
3. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
4. Use immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week. For longer storage, freeze in ice cube trays and transfer to a freezer bag once frozen.


*Source: Moosewood Restaurant New Classics Cookbook*
